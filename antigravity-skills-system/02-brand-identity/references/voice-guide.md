## Punctuation Rules

- **Periods:** Use in body copy, skip in headlines/buttons
- **Exclamation:** Max 1 per screen, only for genuine celebration
- **Ellipsis:** Only for "Loading..." or truncation
- **Emoji:** Sparingly, match brand personality
